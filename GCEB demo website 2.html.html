To provide world class engineers who are ethical and good citizens of our motherland.

Government College of Engineering, Bargur is one of the educational institutions developed by Government of TamilNadu, which was started in the year 1994 to cater to the needs of the nation in the development of technocrats and to provide facilities for educating and training men and women to meet the entrepreneurial and management needs. The TamilNadu Government has created adequate infrastructural facilities and sufficient funds and is keen on developing the institute for higher education.The college has been approved by the AICTE and the college intake is purely based on merit.
